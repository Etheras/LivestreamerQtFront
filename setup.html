LivestreamerQtFront

A Livestreamer front-end created in Qt.

View project on GitHub

Prerequisites

  • Livestreamer
  • Media Player
  • LivestreamerQtFront

Options Menu


  • By clicking the button with "..." under the livestreamer path, point to the livestreamer.exe
  • By clicking the button with "..." under the Media Player path, point to the VLC.exe("player name".exe for other players)

  • By clicking the button with "..." under the OAuth token, authorize with twitch, and when you are redirected to an empty page, copy the token from the URL(should be 30 characters long)
  • Press "Okay"

You are done

Now simply go to File, click Reset and enjoy
